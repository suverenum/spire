# PR FAQ Example: Instant Intercompany Settlement

## 1. Heading

Instant cross-border intercompany settlement for mid-market enterprises

## 2. Subtitle

Move money between your subsidiaries in seconds, not days -- 10x cheaper than SWIFT, with real-time ERP integration.

## 3. Solution (Key Benefits)

Starting today, multi-entity companies can move USDC between subsidiary wallets instantly and for free, with off-ramp to local currency (BRL, MXN, INR) through local rails. Per-entity approval policies are enforced on-chain -- transfers under $10K auto-approved, above $100K requires CFO + CEO. All transactions sync to NetSuite in real-time as standard accounting entries.

## 4. Problem (Target Audience & Evidence)

Mid-market companies ($0.5B-$5B revenue) with subsidiaries across countries lose $1M-$3.5M/year to FX spreads and intermediary fees on emerging-market corridors. SWIFT takes 1-3 business days. ~12,000 companies in the US and Europe have this problem. 23% of large-company CFOs plan treasury crypto use within 2 years. The GENIUS Act (July 2025) and MiCA provide regulatory clarity.

## 5. Problem (Why Us & Success Metrics)

Solving this positions us as the treasury layer for mid-market multinationals -- a $1.8B SAM. We measure success by: number of active treasuries, monthly transaction volume, and off-ramp volume by corridor.

## 6. Customer Quote

"We move $4M/month between our US HQ and Brazil subsidiary. SWIFT costs us $120K-$200K/year in FX spreads alone, and every transfer takes 2 days. If we could settle in seconds at 0.5%, that's $80K-$160K saved annually." -- CFO, mid-market tech company with LATAM operations.

## 7. Out of Scope

- Cards, expense management, invoice processing
- Full accounting (lives in ERP)
- Lending and credit facilities
- SWIFT integration

## 8. How to Get Started

Create a treasury, add your entities, complete KYB via the onboarding flow. Associate wallets with your legal entities. Fund via ACH or SEPA. Start moving money.

## 9. FAQs

**Q: Do I need to understand crypto?**
A: No. The CFO sees USDC balances alongside traditional bank accounts in their ERP. Stablecoin rails are invisible infrastructure.

**Q: What happens if the off-ramp provider goes down?**
A: Funds remain in your on-chain wallet (you control the keys). We integrate multiple providers per corridor for redundancy.

**Q: Is this regulated?**
A: On/off-ramp is handled by licensed payment providers. We operate as an application layer with OFAC sanctions screening and KYB collection. See compliance documentation for details.
